About the Institution

SRM Institute of science and technology is one of the top ranking private institutes in India offering a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programs in Engineering, Management, Medicine and Health sciences, and Science and Humanities. Faculty from abroad, flexible and dynamic curriculum, exciting research and global connections are the features that set SRM apart. Students have a wide choice of cutting edge programs including Computing Technology, GIS, and embedded systems. Most of these courses are offered in close collaboration with abroad universities. Ramapuram Campus is in the heart of the city which is in close vicinity to industrial and commercial hotspots. It has various student committees, forums and clubs which aims in honing their overall skills and providing them with multiple dimensional opportunities.

About the Department

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Ramapuram, is being functioning since 2004. The mission is to evolve and enhance the fundamentals of Computer Science to provide in-depth technical knowledge and opportunities, for the beneficial of students and faculty members. Eminent facilities are available for imparting well renowned education for students. Global Excellence and Local Relevance in research, teaching, and technology development is the vision of our Department. The Department has always been on a high growth path and is keeping pace with the current technological trends. The department of Computer Science and Engineering ensures to provide quality education which fosters creative thinking and encourages innovation among students. The department has Produced 100% placement in every academic year. Regular Guest Lectures by eminent persons provide ample exposure to the student’s latest development in industry. Our alumni have been meritoriously placed in companies all over the world.

About the Conference

The objective of this conference is to bring together researchers and PG Students to explore challenges, issues and opportunities in advanced computing, including distributed systems and recent trends in computer applications. This conference will analyze the emerging issues in recent research in advanced computing and provide a platform to publicize with the latest development in their respective fields.

Call for papers

Papers are invited from research scholars, academicians and UG/PG students. Full Papers not exceeding 6 pages prepared in IEEE format should be sent to the email id ncrrac.2019@srmuniv.ac.in. For each accepted paper to be published in the proceedings, at least one author of the paper has to register for the conference, also send 2 hard copies of the paper to THE CONVENER. The topics of interest include but not limited to the following,

  • Machine Learning
  • Big Data Analytics
  • Neural Networks
  • Cloud Computing
  • Block Chain
  • Soft Computing
  • Web intelligence
  • Ad-hoc & Sensor Networks
  • Data Mining
  • Mobile Computing
  • Distributed Databases
  • Grid Computing
  • Image Processing
  • Machine Learning
